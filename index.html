About Us

Legends are Coming...


Divine Legends is a Solana NFT project, Play-to-Earn Gaming Platform and Branding project focused on development, community growth, innovation and member prosperity. 70% of mint revenue will be used to grow Divine Legends Treasury and to develop exclusive utilities. Every Owner of Divine Legends NFT will have access to all present and future utilities including Airdrop, Staking, $LEGEND and many more. Read more about us in our Whitepaper
